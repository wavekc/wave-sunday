Section

v.3 — God's People Who Shall Be Volunteers in the Beauties of Holiness

3 Your people shall be volunteers In the day of Your power; In the beauties of holiness, from the womb of the morning, You have the dew of Your youth. (NKJV, Ps. 110:3)
3 Your troops will be willing on your day of battle. Arrayed in holy splendor, your young men will come to you like dew from the morning’s womb (NIV, Ps. 110:3)
In the last days, we will not see “a generation that is devoted simply because they are called Christians,” but a generation that is captivated by God’s grace and love and willingly serves Him.
“God, raise up in this generation a willing generation deeply rooted in Your grace, rooted in love! Raise up a generation of love that follows You wherever You go!”
