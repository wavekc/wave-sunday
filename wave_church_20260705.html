Give Us the Fountain of Living Water

With what can the root of the problems in our lives be filled? Jesus turns our eyes from the wells of the world to Himself, the fountain of living water.

Introduction

We are hearing news of endless wars on this earth, earthquakes and natural disasters, and perhaps even other wars that may break out. On the one hand, we hear the opposite kind of news, that the world is getting better and better because of the development of technology. On the other hand, we also cannot hide the anxiety that comes from not being able to keep up with all that technological progress. Where should we be focusing in this generation? Our lives, too, are sometimes far from easy.

Today, we want to meet the Samaritan woman at noon in the Middle East 2,000 years ago. She was carrying the problems she had to bear through her own life, and she was also going through a weary day of having to draw water. Through her, we want to find the answer we need as we live today. With what can the root of the problems in our lives be filled?
